### Introduction

Welcome to **Quranic Journey**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

### Developer Information

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **User Preferences**: Language settings (Indonesian/English)
- **Reading Progress**: Bookmarks, last read position, and reading history
- **Gamification Data**: Symbolic "Pahala Points" and milestone achievements stored locally on your device

#### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Features accessed, time spent reading, verses played
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Local Storage**: Saved Fun Facts and Tadabbur reflections stored on your device

#### 1.3 Third-Party Data
- **AI-Generated Content**: When using the Islamic Fun Facts feature, requests are sent to Google Gemini AI. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for how Google processes this data.

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide Core Features**: Deliver Quran reading, audio playback, and Tadabbur content
- **Personalization**: Remember your language preference and reading progress
- **Generate Content**: Use AI to create relevant Islamic Fun Facts
- **Improve Experience**: Track usage patterns to enhance app features
- **Gamification**: Calculate and display symbolic Pahala Points (stored locally)

### 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Most of your data (reading progress, preferences, Pahala Points, saved Fun Facts) is stored **locally on your device** and is not transmitted to our servers.
- **No User Accounts**: This app does not require user registration or login.
- **Security Measures**: We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, but please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

### 4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

#### 4.1 Google Generative AI (Gemini)
- **Purpose**: Generate Islamic Fun Facts
- **Data Shared**: Your selected language preference and generation requests
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### 4.2 Audio Streaming
- **Purpose**: Stream Quranic recitations
- **Source**: Audio files are downloaded and stored locally on your device for offline playback.
- **Data Shared**: Audio file requests only

### 5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not require registration and stores data locally, there is minimal privacy risk for young users.

### 6. In-App Purchases and Donations

- **Donation Feature**: The app may include a donation/support feature
- **Payment Processing**: All payment transactions are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store
- **No Financial Data Stored**: We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information

### 7. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Data stored on your device remains until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- **No Server Data**: We do not maintain servers that store your personal information

### 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access Your Data**: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app
- **Delete Your Data**: Clear app data or uninstall the app to remove all stored information
- **Opt-Out**: Disable AI Fun Facts feature to avoid third-party AI processing

### 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

### 10. Contact Us
